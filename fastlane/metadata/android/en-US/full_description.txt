Muajjin is a comprehensive Islamic Salat times application designed for Muslims worldwide. It provides accurate Salat times based on your location, along with Hijri dates and Suhoor and Iftar times.

## Key Features

Accurate Salat Times
• Location-based Salat time calculation
• Support for 12+ calculation methods, including:
  - Umm al-Qura University, Makkah
  - University of Islamic Sciences, Karachi
  - Muslim World League
  - Egyptian General Authority of Survey
  - and more
• Shafi'i and Hanafi options for Asr Salat

Hijri Calendar
• Accurate Islamic (Hijri) dates
• Automatic date change at Maghrib
• Manual adjustment for local moon sighting

Saum Times
• Suhoor end countdown
• Iftar start countdown
• Adjustable Suhoor and Iftar times

Prohibited Salat Times
• Shuruq (sunrise) display
• Ghurub (sunset) display
• Zawal indicator

Jama'ah Times
• Custom congregation times for each Salat
• Countdown to Jama'ah
• Clear distinction between Salat start and Jama'ah time

Location Support

• City and country display

Appearance

Privacy Focused

## Salat Time Calculation

Muajjin uses the Adhan library for Salat time calculations. Times are determined based on your location, selected calculation method, chosen Madhab, elevation, timezone, and astronomical data.

## Hijri Calendar

Hijri dates follow the Umm al-Qura calendar system used in Saudi Arabia. The Islamic date changes at Maghrib in accordance with Islamic tradition.

## Permissions

Location Permission: Used only to calculate accurate Salat times. Location data is never shared with third parties.
